ABOUT FC MAPS

FC Maps is an interactive atlas of professional football. The current edition focuses on the FIFA World Cup 2026, plotting where every player in every qualified national squad was born.

The data combines squad lists with geocoded birthplaces, club affiliations, and current season rosters. Each marker on the map represents one player. Click on a flag to see the player's career details.

Data sources. Squad and biographical information was collected from publicly available football references. Birthplace coordinates were derived using the OpenStreetMap Nominatim geocoder, supplemented with manual corrections where the geocoder could not resolve a location (historical countries, alternate spellings, small villages). Country borders come from Natural Earth.

Methodology. Players born in countries that no longer exist (Yugoslavia, Czechoslovakia, Zaire) retain their historical country name as recorded at the time of birth, while the geographic coordinates fall within the modern successor state. Coastal and island birthplaces are matched within a 5 km tolerance of national borders.

Open project. The code, scrapers, and data pipeline are open source. Feedback, corrections, and collaborations are welcome.
